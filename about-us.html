Hazel Nightingale

Yoga Instructor

Hazel was first introduced to yoga at the age of 6 when her mother and father toured around North America in their van and stopped at a yoga festival in Sedona, Arizona. Since 6 she has practiced yoga everyday of her life for 4 hours a day. At the age of 17, she took off and went to India to become a Certified Yoga Instructor. She fell in love with teaching and became one of the head instructors in Rishikesh, India at Yoga-tta Be Kiddin’ Me Institution for the first 5 years of her teaching career. Hazel has taught at various places around the world including India, Indonesia, Thailand, Costa Rica, Mexico, Switzerland, and Italy before settling in Calgary, Canada. She is certified in Vinyasa, Hatha, Yin, Restorative, Bikram, Baptiste, Ashtanga, Iyengar, Kundalini, Prenatal, Anusara, and Jivamaukti.
